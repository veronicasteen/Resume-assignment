Anakin Skywalker

My name is Anakin Skywalker. I am a former Jedi Master/Sith Lord from the desert planet Tatooine. I was discovered as a slave by Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn, who instantly labeled me as the Chosen One. I showed immense Force sensitivity from a very young age. Apparently, my dad is the Force itself.

I was trained under the guidance of Jedi Knight Obi-Wan Kenobi. We parted ways after I started doubting the Jedi Council and began suspecting him of having an affair with my wife. This later ended with Obi-Wan cutting off my limbs, and me ultimately joining the dark side.

I had a brief time as Sith Lord Darth Vader, but after saving my son, I am once again reunited with the Force and looking for new work opportunities as a Force ghost. I am available in any part of the galaxy and ready to redeem myself.
